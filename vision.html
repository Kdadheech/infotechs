Apple Vision Pro is a mixed reality headset that lets you blend digital content with the real world. Here's a rundown of its features:

Mixed Reality Experience:

  • Combines virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) to create a seamless experience where digital objects appear in your physical space.
  • Imagine watching a movie on a giant screen that fills your room, or attending a virtual concert with realistic sights and sounds.

3D Capture:

  • Capture photos and videos in 3D with spatial depth.
  • Relive these moments with a special sense of immersion using the headset's spatial audio features.
  • Even existing photos and videos from your iPhone can be viewed in a new dimension on the Apple Vision Pro.

Cinematic Experience:

  • Watch movies and shows on a virtual giant screen that adjusts to the size of your room.
  • Spatial audio creates a surround sound effect that makes you feel like you're part of the action.

Gaming:

  • Play games in a fully immersive environment.
  • Connect your controller and enjoy a whole new level of gaming experience.

Other Features:

  • Lightweight and comfortable design: Made of a single piece of curved glass and a lightweight aluminum frame.
  • Modular system: For potential future upgrades and customization.
  • Advanced technology: Custom Apple silicon chips, high-resolution displays, and multiple cameras and sensors to create a realistic and responsive experience.

Availability and Pricing:

  • Released in February 2024 in the United States, with a worldwide launch yet to be announced.
  • Starts at US$3,499.
